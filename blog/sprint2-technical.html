Margins, Padding and Borders


What is the difference between margins padding and borders?


The best way to think of these three is to start off with a one box. This box has text in it. The text is the element and the box around it is the border.
The padding is the space between the border and the text. This makes our border bigger around the textarea and lets use manipulate the size of the box and position of the text inside.
The margins is the area around the border. This lets us change how far away the border is away from other elements.
Its a box, in a box, in a another box. A boxception.
